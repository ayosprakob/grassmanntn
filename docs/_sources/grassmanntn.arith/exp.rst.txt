exp()
========================================

.. py:function:: grassmanntn.arith.exp(gnum)

The exponentiation of a Grassmann number

**Parameters:**

- **gnum:** `grassmann_number <grassmann_number.html>`__

  The exponent.

**Returns:**

- **out:** `grassmann_number <grassmann_number.html>`__

  The exponentiation. This is the same as calling ``(numpy.e)**gnum``.
